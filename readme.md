due to popular demand, here are the files for my folding sim rig/wheel stand.

was made to fit me (180cm tall, avg/slender build) with a random thrift shop seat set down in some mounts, so you will need to design your own seat mount or figure out what seat i used and find one somewhere lol (its a great chair)

my hardware is a simagic alpha mini with fanatec clubsport V3 pedals. the wheeldeck can prob support most deck mounted wheelbases, but the pedal deck is made specifically for the clubsport V3 pedals, so you might have to make something yourself if you use different pedal.

file comes with side braces, but after building it, i found out the deck as is can support my full weight (70kg) with practically no deflection even when moving around, so are not really needed.

some of the hardware might be difficult to source locally, especially the large washers and sholder bolts, but aliexpress has pretty much all of the hardware required. i got the brackets/plates/profiles off of aluxprofiel locally.

for the sholder bolts, the sholder is M12 (so 12mm), and the threads will be M10.

for the printed parts, print all the parts out of PLA/ASA with 3.2mm shell (8 perimiters and 16 top/bottom layers on .4 nozzle .2 layer height) and 50% infill for proper strenght, avoid PETG and ABS due to the material being less stiff. if you want absolutely no flex out of the printed parts you can always print with filled filaments, or with CF-PPS if you hate your wallet :)
